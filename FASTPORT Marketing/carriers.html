What is JobMaps™ for Carriers?

Traditional classified job listings stink for trucking. No one can read thousands of poorly drafted, stale, puffing-filled postings to find the one position that fits them. The experience is so disheartening that it chases good candidates away from trucking.

Create Your JobMaps™

The first step is to create your JobMaps™. Enter your company information, your job benefits, and your requirements once. Then draw your JobMap with just a few clicks.

Upgrade Your JobMaps™

If you want your JobMaps™ to really stand-out, create a Custom Pin and distinguish your company from the standard pins on the map. Or, you can also upload your logo to show right on your JobMaps™.

JobMaps™ on Your Website

If you are really interested in looking awesome, you can place your JobMaps™ on your own company website. With just one simple line of code, a trucker can browse every one of your current postings, and no one elses, increasing your chance to attract that right trucker. If you are unhappy with your current website, we can even create one for you.

JobMaps™ Everywhere

To get even more exposure, you can send your JobMaps™ to other sites. Do you like recruiting military veterans? One click sends your postings to the U.S. Chamber of Commerce's Hiring Our Heroes program, who use JobMaps™ as their posting technology. Have you had good luck on Craigslist? One click sends your jobs there. Click here for a list of our favorite partner sites.

Manage JobMaps™ Leads

When truckers find your job that is perfect for them, they can contact you in just a couple clicks. That puts each recruit in the JobMaps™ funnel. Our funnel tracks your recruits process from the initial inquiry, to the job offer, and through the application and onboarding process.

Improve Your JobMaps™

To get even better results, JobMaps™ tracks specific metrics on your job postings. You can see which postings are performing the best and which sources are getting the most recruits.
